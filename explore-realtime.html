Flussi di dati in diretta dalle reti di produzione e trasporto nelle province di Asti e Cuneo.

Agricultural markets move fast. Prices shift overnight, harvests arrive earlier or later than expected, and transport delays can cascade across an entire supply chain within hours. In this environment, access to real-time data is not a luxury — it is a basic operational requirement for anyone involved in the production, movement, or sale of horticultural products.

MOR's real-time data layer was built to meet this need. It aggregates live feeds from multiple sources — GPS sensors on enrolled transport vehicles, market price updates, and production reporting from cooperatives — and presents them in a unified, geographically organized interface that makes the current state of the territory immediately readable.

"Data that arrives too late is not information — it is history. MOR is built around the principle that producers deserve to act on what is happening now."

What Updates in Real Time

The platform currently tracks three categories of real-time information. Vehicle positions — GPS coordinates from enrolled transport vehicles, updated every few minutes, showing the live location of deliveries across the Asti-Cuneo corridor. Market conditions — price and availability updates from participating wholesale markets and cooperatives, helping producers and buyers align on current values before committing to transactions. Production alerts — notifications from cooperative members about harvest readiness, unexpected yield changes, or quality issues that affect supply.

How the Data Is Displayed

All real-time data is displayed as a layer on the MOR interactive map. Vehicle positions appear as moving icons with color coding by product type. Market condition updates appear as municipality-level indicators showing whether current prices are above, at, or below the seasonal average. Production alerts appear as pinned notifications on the relevant farm or cooperative location.

Historical Comparison

One of the most valuable features of the real-time layer is the ability to compare current conditions against historical baselines. A delivery that is running 40 minutes late only becomes meaningful when you know whether that delay is typical for this route at this time of year — or whether it signals something unusual that requires attention.

MOR provides this context automatically, flagging deviations from historical norms and allowing users to drill down into the underlying data to understand the cause. This turns raw real-time data into genuinely actionable intelligence for producers, logistics managers, and market operators.

Looking Ahead

Future development of the real-time layer will incorporate weather data integration — connecting forecast conditions directly to production and transport planning — and expand the network of participating cooperatives and market operators to improve coverage across the full Asti-Cuneo territory.

Aggrega flussi in diretta da molteplici fonti — sensori GPS su veicoli di trasporto aderenti, aggiornamenti dei prezzi di mercato e rendicontazione della produzione dalle cooperative — e li presenta in un'interfaccia unificata e geograficamente organizzata.

Cosa si Aggiorna in Tempo Reale

La piattaforma traccia attualmente tre categorie di informazioni in tempo reale. Le posizioni dei veicoli — coordinate GPS dai veicoli di trasporto aderenti. Le condizioni di mercato — aggiornamenti di prezzi e disponibilità dai mercati all'ingrosso e cooperative partecipanti. Gli avvisi di produzione — notifiche dai soci delle cooperative su prontezza del raccolto, variazioni inattese della resa o problemi di qualità.

Come Vengono Visualizzati i Dati

Tutti i dati in tempo reale vengono visualizzati come layer sulla mappa interattiva MOR. Le posizioni dei veicoli appaiono come icone in movimento con codice colore per tipo di prodotto. Gli aggiornamenti sulle condizioni di mercato appaiono come indicatori a livello comunale. Gli avvisi di produzione appaiono come notifiche fissate sulla posizione rilevante dell'azienda o cooperativa.
